# How we price.

Every product on Boring is sold with a fixed 50% markup over its landed cost. That is the number. It does not change during sales, for members, or by season. When the cost of the product goes up or down, the price moves with it.

We chose 50% because it is the minimum margin required to run a responsible business — support, warranty claims, shipping, returns, and the careful research we do before adding anything to the catalogue. It is not the maximum margin we could charge. It is the one we think is fair.

## What the markup pays for.

— Research and selection. Every product is reviewed against published evidence or financial benchmarks before it is listed.

— Two-year warranty on every physical item. No questions. No receipt hunting.

— Customer support by email, answered by a person.

— Honest comparison against the brands you might otherwise buy.

## What we do not do.

We do not run discounts. We do not inflate prices before a sale. We do not sell attention to brands that want placement. We do not accept payment for favourable coverage. If a product stops meeting its evidence standard, we remove it.

## Members.

Members pay 10% less. The membership is free. It requires an account and notification permission. That is it.

## Referrals.

If you invite someone and they buy, we share part of the margin with you as store credit — 10% of the profit on that order. This is how our best customers fund their next purchase.
